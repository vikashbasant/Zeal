About Us

ZEAL GLOBAL is an established telecom infrastructure contractor and vendor for leading telecom companies across India.

We work as a trusted execution partner for major operators including Jio, Samsung, and other telecom brands, delivering end-to-end infrastructure solutions. Our experienced project execution team combines technical expertise with rigorous quality standards and a commitment to timely delivery.

Every project is executed with a focus on quality, safety, and schedule adherence. We operate from our base in Phase 8 Industrial Area, Mohali, serving clients across Punjab and pan-India for tower installation, fiber rollout, and network deployment.

Our Services

End-to-end telecom infrastructure solutions for operators and enterprises.

Telecom Tower Installation

Full turnkey installation of telecom towers including foundation, mast erection, and antenna mounting for robust network coverage.

Fiber Optic Cable Laying

Professional trenching, duct laying, and fiber optic cable installation for high-speed backhaul and last-mile connectivity.

Network Rollout & Deployment

End-to-end network rollout management including site identification, installation, integration, and commissioning.

4G / 5G Infrastructure Setup

Deployment of 4G and 5G radio equipment, small cells, and supporting infrastructure for next-generation networks.

Telecom Equipment Installation

Installation and commissioning of BTS, RRU, BBU, power systems, and shelter equipment at telecom sites.

Civil & Electrical Works

Civil construction, tower foundation, compound works, and electrical installation including DG and power distribution.

Site Maintenance & Support

Ongoing site maintenance, preventive checks, fault rectification, and technical support to ensure network uptime.
